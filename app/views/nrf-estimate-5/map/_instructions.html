Draw a boundary

Click the 'Draw boundary' button to start drawing your red line boundary on the map. Click on the map to place points and create the outline of your development area. The boundary will automatically snap to building outlines and other features to help with accuracy.

Edit a boundary

Click the 'Edit boundary' button to modify your existing boundary. You can drag points to new positions, add new points by clicking on the boundary line, or remove points by clicking on them. Click 'Done' when you're finished editing.

Delete a boundary

Click the 'Delete boundary' button to remove your current boundary from the map. You can then draw a new boundary if needed.

Navigate the map

Use the search bar to find a location by address or postcode. Use the scroll wheel or pinch gesture to zoom in and out. Click and drag to pan around the map.

Snap to features

When drawing, your cursor will snap to nearby building outlines and land boundaries to help you draw an accurate boundary. Look for the snap indicator to confirm snapping is active.
