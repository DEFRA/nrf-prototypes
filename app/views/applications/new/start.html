Submit a new development application

Use this service to submit details of your proposed housing development and receive a quote for the required environmental levy.

This service will help you:

  • Determine if your development falls within Environmental Development Plan boundaries
  • Calculate the environmental impact costs for your development
  • Receive a detailed quote for the required levy
  • Complete payment for your environmental impact

Before you start

You'll need:

  • Your development site location (postcode, coordinates, or boundary file)
  • The number of houses in your development
  • Information about your wastewater treatment arrangements (if applicable)
  • Pond location details (if required for District Level Licensing)

What happens next

  1. Provide development location - Upload a file, enter postcode, coordinates, or draw on a map
  2. Check EDP boundaries - We'll determine which Environmental Development Plans apply
  3. Provide additional details - Enter house count and other required information
  4. Review quote - See the breakdown of your environmental levy
  5. Make payment - Complete payment through GOV.UK Pay

Environmental Development Plans

Your development may be subject to one or more Environmental Development Plans:

District Level Licensing (DLL)

For developments affecting great crested newt habitats. Requires pond location details.

Nutrient Mitigation

For developments in areas with nutrient pollution concerns. Requires wastewater treatment site selection.
