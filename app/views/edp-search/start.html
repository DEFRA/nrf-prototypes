Development Site Assessment

Use this service to check if your development site falls within Environmental Development Plan (EDP) boundaries and calculate the environmental impact costs you will be required to pay.

This service helps you understand:

  • Whether your development is within District Level Licensing (DLL) boundaries
  • Whether your development is within Nutrient Mitigation boundaries
  • The environmental impact costs for your development
  • Which wastewater treatment sites are available for your development

Before you start

You'll need:

  • Your development site location (postcode, coordinates, or boundary file)
  • The number of houses in your development
  • Information about your wastewater treatment arrangements (if applicable)

How to provide your development site

You can provide your development site location in several ways:

Upload a file

Upload a shape file (.shp), KML file (.kml), or GeoJSON file (.geojson) containing your development boundary.

Enter postcode

Enter the postcode of your development site and draw the boundary on a map.

Enter coordinates

Enter the latitude and longitude coordinates of your development site and draw the boundary.

Draw on map

Use an interactive map to locate and draw your development boundary.
